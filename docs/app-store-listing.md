## Version Information

### Description

del is a companion app for somatic coaching clients. It supports the space between weekly sessions with your coach through daily practices, check-ins, messaging, and a personal journey map.

Features:

- Weekly practice reminders assigned by your coach
- Daily check-ins with text or voice notes
- Private messaging with your coach
- Journey map: session reflections written by your coach
- Streak tracking to build consistency

del is an invite-only app. Your coach sends you an invitation link to get started.

## App Review Information

### Notes for Reviewer

This app is invite-only. A coach invites clients via email link. To test the app:

1. Open the invite link provided in the demo account below
2. Create a password when prompted
3. The app requires an active coaching relationship to display content
